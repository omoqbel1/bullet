Privacy Policy

Effective Date:08/02/2019

Bullets Transport LLC values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information, particularly when you opt into our SMS/text messaging services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, phone number, email address, and physical address.
  • Mobile Information: Phone numbers and opt-in consent for SMS messaging.
  • Website Usage Data: Information on how you interact with our website.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and manage our SMS/text messaging services.
  • To respond to inquiries and provide customer support.
  • To send marketing messages and promotions with your consent.
  • To improve and customize your website experience.

3. SMS/Text Messaging Privacy

  • No Mobile Sharing: Your mobile information will not be shared, sold, or rented to third parties or affiliates for marketing purposes.
  • Support Services: Mobile information may be shared only with subcontractors for support services (e.g., customer service).
  • Opt-In Consent: By providing your phone number, you consent to receive SMS messages related to our services.
  • Opt-Out: You can opt out at any time by texting "STOP" to our shortcode. Reply "HELP" for more assistance.
  • Frequency: You will receive up to 8 messages per month. Message and data rates may apply.

4. How We Protect Your Information

We implement secure measures to protect your personal and mobile information, including encryption, secure servers, and limited access protocols.

5. Your Rights

  • Opt out of SMS/text messaging at any time.
  • Access, correct, or request deletion of your personal information.
  • Contact us with privacy concerns at the details below.
