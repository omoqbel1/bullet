FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know about our transportation services

How long does shipping typically take?

Transit times vary by distance, but most shipments are delivered within 3-7 business days. We provide accurate delivery estimates with your quote and keep you updated throughout the journey.

Is my equipment insured during transport?

Yes! All shipments include basic cargo insurance. We also offer additional coverage options for high-value equipment. Our carriers are fully licensed, bonded, and insured.

What factors affect my shipping cost?

Pricing depends on distance, equipment size/weight, pickup/delivery locations, and current market conditions. We provide transparent, competitive quotes with no hidden fees.

Can I track my shipment in real-time?

Absolutely! You'll receive regular updates throughout transit, and our team is always available to provide the latest status on your shipment. We keep you informed every step of the way.

What types of equipment do you transport?

We transport heavy machinery, construction equipment, agricultural equipment, industrial equipment, and more. From excavators to tractors, we have the expertise to handle specialized cargo safely.

What if I need to change my shipping dates?

We understand that plans change. Contact us as soon as possible, and we'll work with you to reschedule. Flexibility is one of our core values, and we'll do our best to accommodate your needs.
